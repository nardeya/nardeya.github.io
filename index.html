ABOUT

John is a 21 year old composer based in New York City. Having started classical piano at age 4, his original productions heavily incorporate aspects of classical music, electronic music, pop, and rock. In his compositions you will hear elements ranging from orchestral-inspired arrangements, to heavy synthetic beats, hip-hop, sampling, trap, and dreamy soundscapes. John approaches his art as both a musician and a storyteller, balancing clever and catchy motifs with ambiance, mood, and a keen ear for tone and timbre. John is also interested in applying his talents as a composer/sound designer in the media of film and gaming. When he is not composing, John enjoys practicing sketch and improvisational comedy, playing and studying video games, and reading. John currently holds a Bachelor's Degree in Biochemistry from New York University.
